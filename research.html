Research

My research lies in many-body quantum optics and cavity QED, with a focus on collective effects, quantum information processing, and engineered open quantum systems.

Collective Effects in Quantum Optics
Cooperative behaviour of atoms coupled to shared electromagnetic modes.

Rydberg Quantum Gates
Many-body quantum gate design using strongly interacting atomic systems.

Competing Interactions
Emergent phases arising from competing interaction ranges.

Non-reciprocal Quantum Systems
Directional interactions in open quantum systems.
